Top 5 Quarterbacks

QB #5 Jalen Hurts, Oklahoma

From Bama to Oklahoma, Hurts handled his transition with class. He displayed the ultimate professionalism post being benched in the biggest game of his life while producing. 2nd in the NCAA in yards with a 32-8 hurts put up numbers. He had a high completion percentage but alot of it was throws to open wideouts.

Pros

  • Balanced

    • No glaring weaknesses

  • Thrives with simplicity
  • Short crossing routes, underneeth throws
  • Adaptability
  • Mobility
  • 4.59 will get the job done

Cons

  • Accuracy
  • He's no jamarcus russell but at times he misses WR's
  • At times crumbled in big games
  • Not the stongest arm
  • Played in one of the most QB friendly offenses

QB #4 Justin Herbert, Oregon

The Prototype has arrived in the damn building. Standing 6'6 240 the american dream at the QB positon. All while running a 4.68; he brought Oregon from mediocrity back to their pinnacle winning a Rosebowl.

Pros

  • Super Athlete
  • Thrives outside the pocket
  • When he's on the move watchout
  • ROCKET ARM
  • Biggest in the class
  • Mobility

Cons

  • He's not an overt leader
  • IDK if this is a flaw but it gives me a weird feeling
  • Doesn't have amazing touch
  • He will have dimes then really bad duds
  • Misreads defenses at times
  • Not the greatest at moving from read to read

QB #3 Jordan Love, Utah State

A QB in the top from UTAH STATE?? Hold on hold your horses big fella. When I remind you he threw 20 touchdowns to 17 Picks, your probably looking sideyed currently. Pump the breaks he really was on a awful team his 2nd year. He had 7 new offense players and a new head coach. His Sophmore year is a better representation. Throwing 32 TD's and 6 INTS. Also let me make the point out of his 17 picks 9 were on hail mary's or dropped passes. He's the most natural thrower of the football in this class. While running a 4.7 with good size. He had the josh allen tendency to try to superhero due to his team being AWFUL. Every other QB in this list had 4 times the talent around them.

Pros

  • SuperMan Tendency
  • Beautiful arm talent
  • makes some of the most wow throws
  • Accuracy
  • Really can make any throw
  • Super fast release

Cons

  • Stares down one WR often
  • Tends to play hero ball
  • Misreads defenses at times

QB #2 Tua Tagovailoa, Alabama

Tua Tuna idk but this boy nice. If he didn't have his injury history he may be the number one QB in this draft. A natural thrower of the football he does it all. There's just the injury history. He'll do best to probably sit a year to ensure his health.

Pros

  • Good descision maker
  • 8-1 TD - INT ratio
  • Great arm talent
  • Mobile
  • can make any throw
  • Eye manipulation

Cons

  • Size
  • Injury History
  • At times holds the ball too long
  • Instead of throwing the short route or the ball away

QB #1 Joe Burrow

You really won't get much more complete of a prospect. From the numbers, to the leadership, and the winning mentality. He's really the wet dream of qb's. He's deadly accurate and literally get's better under pressure. He's the best qb prospect to come out in recent years foreshore. And he thrived while running the same offense at LSU as the New Orleans saints.

Pros

  • Improvising
  • No such thing as a broken play
  • Great accuracuy from all places
  • IQ he'll make the right throw
  • He's just knows how to leave the pocket at the right time
  • Handles pressure very well
  • Knows how to manipulate Db's
  • He's a guy a team will rally around

Cons

  • Arm strength
  • It's not terrible but its not amazing
  • One of the older rookies
  • Two years as a starter

Top 5 WR's

WR #5 Henry Ruggs, Alabama

at one point he had 5 catches all for touchdowns 4.27 your not catching him he’s a speedster but unlike most speedsters (John ross, desean Jackson) he doesn’t struggle with drops. And is an average route runner who pulls away from dbs with no problem. I think he’s more than just a burner like a John ross but it’ll take the right system to incorporate him. He’s tyreke Hill esk on a bigger frame not as explosive but still can go. He didn’t always create the most separation on his routes at times in college. Which is a bit of a red flag for the next level; but with his shiftiness I believe he can develop into a better route runner. Not a WR one needs to be in a system with another primary option. Gadget player who should be schemed in book or bust week to week type. I think he’s a bit overhyped and you can get a later round WR with speed that can do 90% of what ruggs can. But save on the value but hey lets see.

Pros

  • Speed
  • Solid Hands
  • Will Snag a ball sometimes

Cons

  • Doesn't get a lot of seperation
  • Gadget player
  • Very Boom or bust
  • High draft price

WR #4 Denzel Mims, Baylor

6’3 runs a 4.38 and MOSSES EVERYBODY. Great hands, might have the best catch in tragic ability in the class. He catches passes with dbs hugging him. He’s VERY physical throws dbs off him like a tight end. Which might hurt him at the next level with offensive pass interference. He’s a straight line guy fade balls verticals. He doesn’t run a lot of routes. But that’s ok paired in a offense where he can be the backside WR with one on one coverage. It could be scary in a potent offense. He’s going to be a safety Valve for somebody. But if a team tries to throw him into a #1 role running ￼ intricate routes he won’t succeed

Pros

  • Long AF
  • Dynamic deep threat
  • Outstanding Catch In Traffic
  • Bullies DB's

Cons

  • Limited Route Tree
  • One dimensional
  • A bit too physical at times

WR #3 Laviska shenault jr, Colorado

One of those players you just want to have the pig skin in their hands. But standing at 6’2 he’s the stretched jarvis landry. Had a ability to make people miss but will drop a shoulder. Colorado used him in the wild cat often he’s that special. He reminds me of what cordelle patterson was supposed to be. I like this kid he has one of the better stories in this class. Not the best route runner but if paired as a # 2 WR or letting him move to the slot he can be a problem. He’s versatile can play rb and wr but his route running isn’t top tier. And his 40 was but cheeks but he’s more quick and shifty than fast.

Pros

  • Versitility
  • New age swiss army type of player
  • Solid role player can make instant impact
  • Adaptability

Cons

  • Hasn't shown alot of route running
  • 40 time was -_-
  • jack of many master of none
  • has some boom or bust in him

WR #2 Ceedee Lamb, Oklahoma

this guys is just a man. One of those wide outs who’s going to come down hill and throw a block. The type who if the balls in the air it’s his to have. I love his tenacity, while standing 6’3 190. Same wirey frame as juedy but he’s more physical. Good speed but not elite 4.5 40 proves that one. He’s more quick has good ability to stop on a dime. He’s the type to make a QB more accurate with a big catch radius. He’s just a well balanced receiver the most balanced in this draft. He’s deandre Hopkins esk with his physicality but a better route runner. He doesn’t need to rely on his physical strength to beat a db like d hop. I think he has the highest ceiling to be a consistent producer in this WR class.

Pros

  • Balanced can do it all
  • Contested catch machine
  • quickeness is excellent
  • can be a one or two reciver
  • physical

Cons

  • Straight Line speed

WR #1 jerry jeudy, Alabama

Juedy when nick saben calls you the best route runner ever that’s saying something. His game looks to instantly translate to the pros with his route running. He will win one on one coverage consistently. They had him running routes even the best route runners don’t run in the NFL. He makes defenders fall on tape. Good run after catch, with a great burst. 6’1 190 hes a bit lanky and will need to add some heft to deal with big physical corners. But he’s the most day one ready receiver in the bunch. He’s a mix between OBJ, amari cooper, and a davante adams. He’s hard to find a comp for Bc not many WR’s have his skills in college.

Pros

  • Route Running

    • No glaring weaknesses

  • Shifty
  • Super advance route tree
  • Can make a instant impact
  • Always open type

Cons

  • Wirey
  • He got knocked of the route at times
  • Had some concentration easy drops
